# PREM AUTONOMOUS CO — MONETIZATION BRIEF
**Revenue Engine v1 · Prepared by Hermes CMO · 2026-07-12**

## 1. The one-line offer
Prem Autonomous Co builds and operates **autonomous AI agent teams** that do the work of a department — content, ops, support, lead-gen, and engineering assistance — at the cost of a freelancer. You brief us once; the team runs itself.

## 2. Who buys (ICP)
- **Solo founders & micro-SaaS** (1–10 people) who need a whole function but can't hire.
- **Agencies** that want to resell agent-run deliverables under their own brand.
- **SME operators** (coaches, consultants, e-commerce) drowning in repetitive work.
- **Indie hackers / builders** shipping faster with agent teammates than human contractors.

## 3. Revenue streams (zero-cost to launch)
1. **Packaged agent subscriptions** — fixed monthly fee per "agent team" (see Service Catalog).
2. **Done-with-you builds** — one-time setup + monthly retainer to stand up a custom agent.
3. **Reseller / affiliate program** — agencies earn 20–30% recurring on referred subscriptions.
4. **Lead magnet → consult** — free ROI calculator routes qualified leads to a paid onboarding.
5. **Open-source gravity** — free agent templates (Paperclip/Remotion pipelines) that funnel into paid managed runs.

## 4. Pricing posture
- Land with a **$49/mo starter**, scale to **$499/mo** for a full autonomous team.
- One-time builds: **$990–$4,900** depending on scope.
- Annual plans get 2 months free (default upsell at checkout).
- No seat limits — price by *outcome throughput*, not headcount. This is the differentiator vs. traditional SaaS.

## 5. Funnel (all free channels)
```
Free template / ROI calculator
        ↓ (email capture)
Welcome sequence (3 emails)
        ↓
Book a 15-min fit call  →  Starter plan or Build proposal
        ↓
Onboarding → activation → monthly recurring
        ↓
Referral / case study → upsell to Team plan
```

## 6. 90-day financial target (conservative)
- Month 1: 5 starter subscribers = **$245/mo**
- Month 2: 15 subs + 1 build = **~$1,240/mo**
- Month 3: 35 subs + 2 builds + 1 reseller = **~$3,200/mo**
- Burn: **$0** (no ad spend; all organic + product-led).
