## Rules that keep it free AND effective
- 25–50 personalized sends/day max. Quality > volume.
- Never paste the same message to 100 people.
- Every touch ends with the calculator or a call, not a pitch deck.
- Track replies in a simple sheet; 1 in 20 reply rate is healthy.

## Objection handlers

  repetitive 80% and report to you. 14-day money-back pilot."
- "We use [tool]." → "Great, our agents plug into your stack; we're the operator, not a replacement."
