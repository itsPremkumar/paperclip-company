## 1. Overview

A 90-second weekly video newsletter template designed for content creators who want to convert their written newsletter into a video format. Each episode follows a 3-segment structure — **News Bite → Deep Dive → Quick Win** — that mirrors the rhythm of a written newsletter but optimized for video. The template includes an intro sting, recurring segment markers, and an outro with an email capture CTA, making it feel like a TV show episode.

**What the customer gets:**
- Recurring intro/outro sting (brand-building — audience recognizes the format week over week)
- 3-segment script structure with fill-in-the-blank slots for each weekly topic
- Segment transition animations (act breaks that feel like a professional broadcast)
- Cutdown config for vertical shorts (9:16 — promote each segment as a standalone TikTok/Reel)
- Theme/music system with episode number overlays (audience sees "Episode 47" and feels FOMO if they miss it)

**Why $29?** A video newsletter is one of the highest-retention content formats in 2025, but producing one weekly is a production nightmare. This template encodes a repeatable 90-minute-production-to-90-second-video pipeline. The customer writes their newsletter content into the slots, the AVG renders the episode, and they publish it to YouTube and Substack simultaneously. No editing, no design, no showrunning experience required.

## 3. Script Template (Fill-in-the-Blank)

> **Instructions for the customer:** Each week, fill in the 3 segments. The intro/outro are standardized and don't change week to week (brand consistency).  
> **TTS voice recommendation:** Pick ONE voice and STICK WITH IT across all episodes. `en-US-JennyNeural` (warm, newsletter-native feel) or `en-US-ChristopherNeural` (deep, podcasty).  
> **Speaking rate:** 1.05x (snappy but clear — newsletter audiences are used to reading fast).

### Batch Render for Season

```bash
# Render 4 episodes in one go (auto-increment episode number)
for ep in 47 48 49 50; do
  avg render --inputProps "{\"episodeNumber\":$ep,\"publishDate\":\"2026-07-$((12 + ep - 47))\"}" \
    --outDir "./output/newsletter/episode-$ep"
done
```

## 9. Premium Pro Tips (Bonus)

1. **Batch Filming Day:** Fill all 4 weekly scripts on Sunday. Render all 4 episodes at once with the batch render command. Schedule-upload to YouTube. One hour of work covers a month of content.
2. **Episode Number FOMO:** The episode counter ("Episode 47") creates a psychological scarcity effect — viewers feel compelled to watch from the beginning. Never reset the counter.
3. **Segments as Shorts:** Each 25–30s segment is a standalone vertical short. That's 3 shorts per episode, 12 shorts per month, all auto-generated from the same script fill.
4. **Music as Brand:** Use the exact same intro music every episode. By episode 10, viewers will hear the first 3 notes and know it's your show. This is called "sonic branding."
5. **YouTube Chapters:** The 5 scenes map directly to YouTube chapters. AVG can output the chapter markers file:
   ```
   0:00 Intro
   0:05 News Bite
   0:30 Deep Dive
   1:00 Quick Win
   1:20 Outro
   ```
6. **Email Capture in Description:** Always include the newsletter signup link in the YouTube video description AND as the pinned comment. Substack report that video descriptions account for 18% of new subscriber conversions.
