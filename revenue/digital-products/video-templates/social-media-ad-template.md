## 3. Script Template (Fill-in-the-Blank)

> **Instructions for the customer:** Write ONE script below; the AVG pipeline auto-generates three lengths by trimming scenes.  
> **TTS voice recommendation:** `en-US-GuyNeural` for authoritative ads, `en-US-JennyNeural` for lifestyle/wellness.  
> **Speaking rates:** 15s = 1.25x (fast), 30s = 1.1x (moderate), 60s = 1.0x (natural).

> **IMPORTANT — Platform Safe Zones:**
> - **9:16 (Stories/TikTok):** Keep text in the middle 60% of the frame (avoid camera notch area top 10% and system UI bottom 15%)
> - **1:1 (Feed):** Text must fit within center 80% (outer 10% on each side is cropped in different feed positions)
> - **16:9 (Facebook/YouTube):** Full frame is usable

### 15-SECOND SCRIPT (0:00–0:15) | Words: ~35–40

> **Structure:** Hook → Problem → Solution → CTA (no feature detail — speed is everything)

> **[VOICEOVER]**
> "`[Hook — 2-3s]`. Tired of `[pain point]`? `[Product Name]` gives you `[core benefit]` in seconds. Available now. `[CTA — "Get it today"]`."

**Scene Variables (15s only):**
- `[Hook]` — short, punchy, question or provocative statement (e.g., "Overpaying for analytics?")
- `[pain point]` — single pain, conversational
- `[Product Name]` + `[core benefit]` — product + one benefit
- `[CTA]` — imperative verb phrase (e.g., "Download now", "Shop the sale", "Start free")

---

### 30-SECOND SCRIPT (0:00–0:30) | Words: ~60–70

> **Structure:** Hook → Problem → Solution (1 feature) → Social Proof → CTA

> **[VOICEOVER]**
> "`[Hook — 3-4s]`. Most `[audience]` struggle with `[pain point]`, wasting `[time/money]` on `[old solution]`. But `[Product Name]` changes everything. With `[key feature]`, you can `[specific benefit]`. Just `[simple action]` and you're done. Join `[number]` happy customers. `[CTA]`."

**Scene Variables (30s):**
- Same as above plus:
- `[audience]` — target demographic (e.g., "small business owners")
- `[old solution]` — what they use today
- `[key feature]` + `[specific benefit]` — one feature-benefit pair
- `[simple action]` — frictionless step
- `[number]` — social proof count ("5,000+")

### 60-SECOND SCRIPT (0:00–1:00) | Words: ~120–140

> **Structure:** Hook → Problem (expanded) → Solution → 2 Features → Social Proof → CTA

> **[VOICEOVER]**
> "`[Hook — 4-5s]`. Here's the reality: `[audience]` are spending `[pain point detail]`. Why? Because `[root cause]`. But `[Product Name]` is different. It `[core mechanism]`, which means you `[key benefit]`.

> "Feature one — `[feature 1 name]`. `[feature 1 description]`.  
> Feature two — `[feature 2 name]`. `[feature 2 description]`.

> "Trusted by `[number]` customers. Ready to start? `[CTA]`."

**Scene Variables (60s):**
- All above plus:
- `[pain point detail]` — quantify the pain (e.g., "10 hours a week on manual reports")
- `[root cause]` — why existing solutions don't work
- `[core mechanism]` — how the product works
- `[feature 1/2 name]` + `[feature 1/2 description]` — two feature-benefit pairs

```bash
# Render all 9 variants in one command
for variant in "60s:16:9" "60s:1:1" "60s:9:16" "30s:16:9" "30s:1:1" "30s:9:16" "15s:16:9" "15s:1:1" "15s:9:16"; do
```

## 8. Premium Pro Tips (Bonus)

1. **Ad Fatigue Prevention:** Generate 5 variants of the hook text (using different `[Hook]` fill values) and batch-render them. Rotate these ad variants weekly to prevent frequency fatigue on Meta.
2. **Captions Are Non-Negotiable:** 85% of social ads play without sound. Burn in every subtitle and make sure the hook is readable in 0.5s without sound.
3. **Text-to-Video Ratio Rule:** For 15s ads, the first 2 seconds must have NO text reading (hook image only) — Meta penalizes ads with dense text in the first frame.
4. **Platform-Specific CTA Text:**
   - TikTok: "Get it →" or "Shop now →"
   - Facebook: "Learn more →" (best CTR)
   - Instagram: "Swipe up →" or "Link in bio"
   - LinkedIn: "Read more →" (professional tone)
5. **Thumbnail / Cover Frame:** Render frame 2 (not frame 0 — skips any fade-in) as the video thumbnail for each variant. Use the hook text overlay as the thumbnail copy.
6. **Fast Start Flag:** Enable `-faststart` (moov atom at front) for all MP4s — social ad platforms reject files that buffer before playing.
