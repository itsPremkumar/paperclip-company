## 30-second verdict

- **Pick Canva if** you want the fastest path from "I need a post / logo / deck" to "it's done," with templates that assume you are not a designer — best for solopreneurs shipping content every day.
- **Pick Figma if** you are building a real product or brand system and need versioned files, component libraries, and developer hand-off — best for founders who treat design as code.
- **Pick Adobe Express if** you already live in the Adobe world and want a lightweight, AI-assisted quick-editor for social + video without opening the full Creative Cloud — best for Adobe refugees.
- **Pick Designs.ai if** you want an end-to-end AI brand kit (logo, video, mockups, copy) generated from a prompt in minutes — best for launching a look before you hire a designer.

None of these is "the thing that grows the business" on its own — a design tool is the *production layer*, and it only pays off when a [content repurposing engine](./content-repurposing-engine-2026.md) feeds it raw material and a [faceless content channel](./faceless-ai-content-channel.md) turns the output into reach. It sits one layer *above* the [image generators](./midjourney-vs-dalle-vs-stable-diffusion-vs-flux-2026.md) and [presentation makers](./gamma-vs-beautiful-ai-vs-tome-vs-slidesai-2026.md) that already live in your stack.

## How to choose without overthinking

Start from the job, not the feature list:

1. **"I post 5 times a week and have zero design hours."** Canva — the template + Magic Studio resize flow means one asset becomes a carousel, a story, and a thumbnail before your coffee cools.
2. **"I'm shipping an app or a real brand and devs need specs."** Figma — components, auto-layout, and hand-off are why every [AI company build guide](./how-to-run-ai-company-zero-budget.md) treats it as the design source of truth.
3. **"I want Adobe polish but not the Creative Cloud price tag."** Adobe Express — Firefly fill and the familiar UX without opening Photoshop for a 30-second fix.
4. **"I need a whole brand yesterday — logo, palette, video, copy."** Designs.ai — prompt in your business and get a coherent starter kit you can refine instead of starting from a blank artboard.

If you're running lean, the right call is the cheapest plan that lets you batch-produce assets and resize them for every channel. The tool is a funnel layer, not the product — spend your energy on the [faceless channel](./faceless-youtube-vs-tiktok-vs-newsletter-2026.md) that distributes the output and the [lead-generation system](./build-ai-lead-generation-system-2026.md) that turns viewers into buyers.
