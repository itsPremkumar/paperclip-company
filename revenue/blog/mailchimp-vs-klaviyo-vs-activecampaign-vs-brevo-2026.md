## 30-second verdict

- **Pick Mailchimp if** you want the lowest-friction entry point with the biggest template library and you're still figuring out what you'll email about — best for beginners and early-stage list building.
- **Pick Klaviyo if** you run (or plan to run) a store and want predictive, event-based flows that tie email + SMS to purchase behavior — best for e-commerce revenue.
- **Pick ActiveCampaign if** you treat email as one arm of a full CRM + automation engine and need branching logic, lead scoring, and deal tracking — best for funnel-driven businesses.
- **Pick Brevo (ex-Sendinblue) if** you want transactional + marketing email, SMS, and WhatsApp in one cheap plan with a generous free tier — best for lean operators watching every dollar.

None of these is "the thing that prints money" on its own — email is the owned channel that compounds. It sits one layer *under* the [newsletter platforms](./beehiiv-vs-substack-vs-convertkit-vs-ghost-2026.md) we already compared, and it only pays off when a [lead-generation system](./build-ai-lead-generation-system-2026.md) feeds it clean contacts.

## How to choose without overthinking

Start from the job, not the feature list:

1. **"I'm brand new and just need to start capturing emails."** Mailchimp — the free tier and template library mean you ship a signup form today, not next week.
2. **"I sell products and want abandoned-cart + browse flows."** Klaviyo — its event-based automations are purpose-built to recover revenue you're already leaving on the table.
3. **"Email is one step in a bigger funnel with leads, scores, and deals."** ActiveCampaign — you get the CRM and branching logic that the pure ESPs don't.
4. **"I need cheap email + SMS + transactional in one place."** Brevo — the unlimited-contacts free tier and volume pricing are the friendliest to a [zero-budget AI company](./how-to-run-ai-company-zero-budget.md).

If you're running lean, the right call is usually the cheapest plan that lets you connect a form and one automated welcome sequence. The tool is a funnel layer, not the product. Spend your energy on the [content repurposing engine](./content-repurposing-engine-2026.md) that fills the list and the [faceless channel](./faceless-ai-content-channel.md) that drives top-of-funnel traffic.
