# PARTNERSHIPS — RESELLER & AFFILIATE PROGRAM
**Turn happy customers and agencies into a zero-cost sales force.**

## Two tracks

### 1. Affiliate (no contract)
- 25% recurring commission on every referral's subscription, for 12 months.
- Unique link + dashboard. Paid monthly via Stripe/Razorpay.
- Best for: founders, creators, community builders.

### 2. Reseller / Agency (contract)
- 30% recurring + white-label option (your brand, our agents).
- Co-marketing: we feature you in the partner directory.
- Best for: agencies, consultants, outsourced-ops shops.

## Why partners say yes
- They sell "AI agent teams" without building anything.
- Recurring income on clients they already serve.
- We handle delivery, support, and uptime — they keep the relationship.

## Onboarding (zero-cost)
1. Apply at prem-autonomous.co/partners (free form).
2. Get link/dashboard + one-pager + pricing sheet (this kit).
3. Send traffic → we close + operate → partner gets paid.

## Partner one-liner
> "Earn 25–30% recurring by offering autonomous agent teams under your brand.
> We build, run, and support them. You own the client."

## Guardrails
- No commission for self-referrals or stacked discounts.
- 30-day hold on first payout (fraud buffer).
- Partners must not promise custom SLAs we don't offer.
