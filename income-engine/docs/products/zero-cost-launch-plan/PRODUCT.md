# 🚀 30-Day Zero-Cost Business Launch Plan
## From Zero to First Paying Customer in 30 Days — $0 Spent

*The exact day-by-day launch roadmap we used to start Prem Autonomous Co on free tools alone. No ad budget, no LLC-before-you-earn theater — just a sequenced plan that gets a real product in front of real buyers in 30 days.*

---

## 📅 Week 1: Foundation (Days 1–7)

| Day | Task | Deliverable |
|-----|------|-------------|
| 1 | Define your offer & target customer | ICP document |
| 2 | Choose your platforms (free) | Platform checklist |
| 3 | Set up your tooling stack | Working dev environment |
| 4 | Create your first asset | Core deliverable draft |
| 5 | Set up your storefront | Gumroad/Ko-fi shop |
| 6 | Build your showcase page | GitHub Pages launch |
| 7 | Review + prepare Week 2 | Week 1 retrospective |

## 📅 Week 2: Create Assets (Days 8–14)

- **Day 8–9:** Write your first 3 product descriptions
- **Day 10–11:** Create product files (scripts, templates, guides)
- **Day 12:** Set up automated delivery (Gumroad handles this)
- **Day 13:** Write your LinkedIn launch post
- **Day 14:** Review pricing + positioning

## 📅 Week 3: Launch (Days 15–21)

- **Day 15:** 🚀 **LAUNCH DAY** — Publish all products
- **Day 16:** LinkedIn launch post + tag connections
- **Day 17:** Cold outreach — send 20+ messages
- **Day 18:** Follow up on opens/replies
- **Day 19:** Join relevant communities + share value
- **Day 20:** Publish case study / sample work
- **Day 21:** Analyze first week stats

## 📅 Week 4: Growth (Days 22–30)

- **Day 22–23:** Double down on best-performing channel
- **Day 24–25:** Iterate pricing based on feedback
- **Day 26:** Launch referral/affiliate program
- **Day 27:** Record first customer testimonial
- **Day 28:** Publish content piece (blog/video)
- **Day 29–30:** Month 1 review + Month 2 plan

## 🧰 What You Need

- **Time:** ~2 hours/day
- **Money:** $0 (all platforms free)
- **Skills:** Basic writing, willingness to learn

---

## 🛡️ Lessons We Baked Into the Plan

- **Foundation before flash.** Week 1 is boring on purpose — a clear ICP and a working storefront beat a pretty logo every time.
- **Ship the asset before the pitch.** By Day 4 you have a draft; by Day 12 delivery is automated. You can't launch what doesn't exist.
- **Launch is a week, not a day.** Day 15 opens the door; Days 16–21 walk people through it. Outreach + community is the engine, not a one-post Hail Mary.
- **Double down, don't spread out.** Week 4 says pick your best channel and pour the remaining hours there. Spreading 2 hrs across 6 platforms = 0 traction.
