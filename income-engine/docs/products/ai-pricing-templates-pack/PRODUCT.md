## 📋 What Each Tier Template Includes

Every tier is a complete offer skeleton, not just a number:

- **Name & positioning statement** — the one-liner that frames the buyer's mindset.
- **Feature checklist** — scoped to exactly what that tier gets (no accidental scope creep).

## 🧮 Quick Math — Annual Discount

```
Monthly = $99
Annual at 2-months-free = $99 × 10 = $990
Equivalent monthly = $990 / 12 = $82.50
Discount vs paying monthly = (99 − 82.50) / 99 ≈ 16.7%  →  quote "≈17% off"
```

## 🛡️ Guardrails We Learned the Hard Way

- **Never sell "unlimited."** Quotas exist to protect you. The moment a tier says unlimited, your worst client will test it.
- **Response-time SLA must be keepable.** 24h is fine; "1h" is a trap unless you have coverage.
- **Annual discount is a closing tool, not a default.** Lead with monthly, offer annual as the savvy-buyer upgrade.
- **Scope the feature checklist before the price.** Price follows scope; scope must never follow a prospect's wish list.
