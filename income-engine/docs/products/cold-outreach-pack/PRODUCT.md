# The 4-step system (recap)

**1. Research before you write.** Never open the compose box without a one-line note on *why this person*. Pull a specific detail: a recent launch, a job post, a post they wrote. No detail → skip them. Targeting beats volume.

**2. Lead with the cost of inaction.** Open on the pain, not on you. *"Most teams your size lose 10–15 hrs/week to [task]"* lands harder than *"We help companies."* Then offer the smallest possible next step: a 15-min call or a free teardown — never a demo-of-everything.

**3. Sequence, don't single-shot.** Plan three touches across 10 days:

| Touch | Day | Goal |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | 0 | Open with specificity + one-line offer |
| 2 | 4 | Nudge with the ROI/proof angle |
| 3 | 9 | Break-up note ("should I close the loop?") |

The break-up email is the highest-reply touch in the sequence. Don't skip it.

**4. Keep volume human.** Send 10–25/day per identity, not 500. Replies scale with relevance, not with spam filters. An agent can draft and personalize; a human owns the mailbox and the send. **Money and sending stay human — the agent only prepares.**

# Template 1 — Email 1 (Founder, Day 0)

```
Subject: [TRIGGER] at [COMPANY]

Hi [FIRST_NAME],

Saw [SPECIFIC DETAIL — launch/post/hire] and it made me think of the
[TEAM SIZE]-person teams we work with. Most lose 10–15 hrs/week to [PAIN]
because [ROOT CAUSE].

We put together a free [TEARDOWN/CALCULATOR] that shows exactly where that
time goes — want me to send it over?

[YOUR_NAME]
```

# Personalization bracket system

Replace these in every send — 30 seconds, not 30 minutes:

- `[COMPANY]` — their company name
- `[FIRST_NAME]` — first name only (never full name in the opener)
- `[TRIGGER]` — the recent event you're referencing
- `[SPECIFIC_DETAIL]` — the one-line "why this person"
- `[PAIN]` — the cost-of-inaction problem
- `[ROOT_CAUSE]` — why it happens
- `[OUTCOME]` / `[TIMEFRAME]` — the proof angle
- `[TEARDOWN/CALCULATOR]` — the asset you point at (never "book a call" vapor)

**Rule:** if you can't fill `[SPECIFIC_DETAIL]`, skip the prospect. Relevance is the whole game.

---

# Volume guardrails (stay out of spam)

- 10–25 sends/day per identity — not 500.
- One identity per real mailbox; warm it up before volume.
- Plain-text first send; no tracking pixels, no 10-link blasts.
- Space touches 0 / 4 / 9 days. Don't compress.
- Replies scale with relevance, not with send count.

---

> **The honest part:** outreach earns the conversation, it doesn't close it. It works best when it points at a real asset (a calculator, a teardown, a product) instead of "book a call" vapor. Automate the *drafting*; stay human on the *sending* and the *money*.
