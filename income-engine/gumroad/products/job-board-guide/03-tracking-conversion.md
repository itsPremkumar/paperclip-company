# Chapter 3 — Tracking & Conversion

What gets measured gets closed.

### Spreadsheet Template (columns)
| Date | Platform | Contact | Persona | Message # | Replied? | Stage | Value | Next Action |

### Response Rate Benchmarks
- LinkedIn DM: 8–15% reply (personalized).
- Naukri/Wellfound: 3–6% response.
- Upwork: 10–20% interview rate on tailored proposals.
- Cold email: 5–10% reply.

### Conversion Optimization Tips
- Reply within 4 hours — speed beats polish.
- Lead with the smallest yes ("15-min call?") not the contract.
- Every reply gets a one-line recap of fit + next step.

### Client Qualification Framework
- **Budget signal:** asks price early = qualified.
- **Authority:** can they say yes alone?
- **Urgency:** deadline mentioned = hot.
- **Fit:** matches a packaged team or product = easy close.
- Score 1–4; only pursue 3+.
