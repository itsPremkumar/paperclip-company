# Chapter 2 — Outreach Strategies

A repeatable system that works without spending a rupee on paid listings.

### Day-by-Day Outreach Schedule
- **Daily:** 10 new profile views + 5 personalized messages (cap to avoid spam filters).
- **Mon/Wed/Fri:** LinkedIn DMs to founders (Persona A/C).
- **Tue/Thu:** Naukri/Wellfound applications + Upwork proposals.
- **Weekly:** 1 value post (case study or tip) to stay visible.

### Message Templates (per platform)

**LinkedIn DM**
"Hey {{name}} — loved your post on {{topic}}. We automate exactly that with agent teams (no headcount). Worth a 15-min chat?"

**Naukri/Wellfound note**
"Available for {{skill}} freelance work — shipped {{result}} for a similar startup. 7-day delivery, $0 upfront on small scopes. Open to a quick call?"

**Upwork proposal**
"Your {{job}} task maps to a workflow I've built 3×. I'll deliver {{deliverable}} in {{time}}. Here's a live example: {{link}}."

### Follow-Up Cadence
- Touch 1: initial message.
- Touch 2 (Day 4): add value (article/tip).
- Touch 3 (Day 9): soft close ("still open to helping?").
- Stop on reply or after 3 touches.

### Response Rate Optimization
- Personalize the first line from their recent activity (2× reply rate).
- Send Tue–Thu, 9–11am local time.
- Keep first message under 60 words.
