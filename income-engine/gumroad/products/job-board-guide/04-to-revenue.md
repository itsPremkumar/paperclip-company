# Chapter 4 — From Outreach to Revenue

Turn a reply into a paying client.

### First Call / Video Prep
- 3 questions: goal, current process, deadline.
- Demo the closest packaged team or product live.
- End with one concrete next step + date.

### Proposal Template
```
To: {{client}}
Re: {{outcome}} in {{timeframe}}
Scope: {{deliverable}}
Price: ${{amount}} (or {{plan}}/mo)
Timeline: {{start}} → {{end}}
Next: approve → I start {{start}}
```

### Pricing Conversation Scripts
- "I price by throughput, not hours — so you pay for outcomes."
- "Start with the $129 team; upgrade only when ROI is clear."
- "Custom build is $2k–$10k, 50% upfront — but the $9 product proves the approach first."

### Onboarding Workflow
1. Sign (free e-sign: Documenso / HelloSign free tier).
2. Shared doc with scope + heartbeat cadence.
3. First deliverable in 7 days; weekly digest after.
4. Collect testimonial at day 30.

### Sample Profile Headline
"AI Automation Engineer | I Build Autonomous Systems for Startups | $0-Investment, 7-Day Delivery"
