# Chapter 1 — Profile Optimization

Your profile is your storefront. Optimize before you send a single message.

### Platform-Specific Profile Templates

**LinkedIn**
- Headline: "AI Automation Engineer | I Build Autonomous Systems for Startups | $0-Investment, 7-Day Delivery"
- About: 3 lines — what you do, who you help, proof (GitHub stars / MRR).
- Featured: pin your best repo + one-pager.

**Naukri.com**
- Headline: "AI/Automation Freelancer — Python, Agents, Workflows"
- Skills: list 15 concrete skills (Python, n8n, Remotion, prompt design…).
- Summary: "I deliver production-ready automation in 7 days at $0 upfront for small scopes."

**Wellfound / YC Work at a Startup**
- Pitch: 2 sentences on the one problem you solve + a link to live demo.

**Upwork / Freelancer / PeoplePerHour**
- Title: "I will build your AI automation or agent workflow"
- Portfolio: 3 before/after examples.

### Headline Formulas That Get Clicks
- "[Outcome] for [audience] | [differentiator]"
- "I [verb] [thing] so you don't have to | [proof]"
- "Former [X] now [Y] — help[ing] [audience] [outcome]"

### Skills Section Optimization
- Lead with the 5 highest-demand skills for your niche.
- Add 2 "rising" skills (agents, RAG, Remotion) to surface in filters.

### Portfolio Showcase Strategy
- One live demo beats ten descriptions.
- Use GitHub Pages or a free Vercel deploy. Link everywhere.
