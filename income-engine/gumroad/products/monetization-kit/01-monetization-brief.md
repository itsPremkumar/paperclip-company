# Asset 1 — Monetization Brief

**Problem:** Founders spin up AI tools but never turn them into recurring revenue.
**Solution:** A tight monetization engine built on free platforms, priced by throughput not seats.

### ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)
- Solo founders, indie hackers, and small agencies running or launching AI-native services.
- Budget: $0 to start, willing to pay $129–$499/mo once value is proven.
- Channels: LinkedIn, Naukri, Wellfound, Upwork, GitHub, Product Hunt.

### 5 Revenue Streams
1. **Agent Team Subscriptions** — packaged agent teams at $129–$299/mo.
2. **Autonomous Team Bundle** — full-stack build at $499/mo.
3. **Custom Builds** — fixed-scope projects, $2k–$10k.
4. **Digital Products** — templates, playbooks, prompt packs (one-time $9–$29).
5. **Reseller/Affiliate** — 25% recurring commission on referred subscriptions.

### Pricing Posture
- Anchor high ($499 bundle), sell the $129–$299 teams.
- All prices by throughput (tasks/mo), never per-seat.
- Annual = 2 months free. No free tier on paid teams (use free content as the lead magnet).

### Marketing Funnel
Free content (SEO articles, GitHub) → Lead magnet (ROI calculator) → Email sequence → Call/DM → Paid team or product.

### 90-Day Target
- **Goal:** $3,200/mo MRR at $0 burn.
- Month 1: publish content + capture 200 leads.
- Month 2: convert 12 teams ($129–$299) + 40 product sales.
- Month 3: add 1 bundle + 2 resellers.
