# Asset 5 — Lead Magnet: AI Agent ROI Calculator

**Landing page copy block**
> "How much could autonomous agents save your business? Plug in your team size, hourly rate, and repeatable tasks. Get your 12-month savings estimate in 10 seconds — no email required to see the number, email to get the full PDF plan."

**Email capture sequence**
1. Welcome (instant): "Here's your ROI snapshot — now here's how to capture it."
2. Day 1: Case study — how we hit $3,200/mo MRR at $0 burn.
3. Day 3: The 6 agent teams, explained.
4. Day 5: Objection handler — "but I need human oversight" (you do, here's the guardrail).
5. Day 7: Soft pitch — book a call or grab a starter team.

**Follow-up automation triggers**
- Opened but no click → send Day 3 early.
- Clicked pricing → tag "hot", route to sales DM.
- Downloaded PDF → add to product retargeting list.
