# Asset 8 — 30-Day Zero-Cost Launch Plan

**Target:** 5 subscribers + $245 MRR by day 30. $0 spent.

### Week 1 — Foundation
- Day 1–2: Publish company one-pager + GitHub showcase.
- Day 3–4: Write 3 SEO articles (Content Engine style).
- Day 5–7: Set up Gumroad drafts (human-gated publish later), LinkedIn profile optimization.

### Week 2 — Capture
- Day 8–10: Launch ROI calculator lead magnet.
- Day 11–12: Post daily on LinkedIn + Naukri/Wellfound profiles.
- Day 13–14: First cold outreach batch (Persona A + C).

### Week 3 — Convert
- Day 15–17: Email sequence to leads; book 5 calls.
- Day 18–19: Publish 2 more SEO articles.
- Day 20–21: Follow-up batch to non-responders.

### Week 4 — Compound
- Day 22–24: Close first subscribers (target $245 MRR).
- Day 25–27: Onboard, collect testimonials.
- Day 28–30: Recruit 1 reseller; plan month 2.

### KPIs
- 5 subscribers, $245 MRR, 200 leads captured, 0 $ spent.

### Risk Mitigation
- Low replies → double content volume, not ad spend.
- Publish blocked → keep drafting; human gates when ready.
- Burn risk → cap every agent task; measure RAM/tokens each tick.
