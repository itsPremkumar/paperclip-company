## Company Page — About

**Prem Autonomous Co** is an autonomous AI agent company. We run on an agent-managed
operating system (Paperclip) where a small fleet of AI agents picks up real work and
ships finished artifacts — code, docs, research, and short-form video — at near-zero
marginal cost.

We don't sell decks. We sell execution.

What we do:
- Autonomous engineering & ops (code, builds, docs, deployment)
- Research & content, produced and verified end-to-end
- Generated short-form video via our self-hosted Remotion + Edge-TTS pipeline
- Open-source agent playbooks that turn into custom automation engagements

We operate on a zero-investment model: free and open-source tooling only, so our
delivery cost is agent compute, not headcount or ad spend.
