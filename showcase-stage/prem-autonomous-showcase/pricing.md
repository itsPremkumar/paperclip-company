# Prem Autonomous Co — Service & Pricing Tiers

> Founder-finalized tier sheet for agent-labor subscriptions. All plans are delivered by
> autonomous agent teams at ~$0 marginal cost (agent compute only). No seat limits.
>
> _Owner: Premkumar M (founder). Canonical source: `revenue/public-pricing-sheet.md`.
> Founder approval required before quoting exact public numbers._

---

## 1. Service definition — "agent labor"

We sell finished, reviewed **deliverables**, not "access to an AI." A client briefs us once;
an autonomous agent team produces the work, self-reviews it, and reports weekly. Categories:

| Capability | Example deliverables |
|------------|----------------------|
| Content | Short-form video scripts, blog posts, newsletters, social posts, Medium articles |
| Ops | Client intake flows, delivery SLA playbooks, process docs, dashboard setup |
| Lead-gen | Cold outreach sequences, free job-board monitoring, CRM hygiene |
| Support | Tier-1 reply drafting, ticket triage, knowledge-base articles |
| Exec | Weekly activity reports, roadmap notes, market/competitor research |
| Engineering | Custom agent/automation build, Remotion video pipelines, CLI tools |

## 2. Subscription tiers (USD / month)

|------|-------|-------------------|----------|

| **Ops Autopilot** | $199 | 10 + workflows | e-commerce / service ops teams |

| **Autonomous Team** | $499 | any 3 plans + CoS | Teams wanting a full pod |

**Free tier (acquisition):** 1 deliverable/month, agent-reviewed, no card. Converts to paid.

**Add-ons (optional):** rush 24h (+$49), white-label report (+$29/mo), dedicated agent slot (+$99/mo).

Annual billing: 2 months free. Resellers: 25% recurring commission. Pilot: 14-day money-back
on first month of any subscription. No seat limits — price is per agent team, not per user.

## 4. Guardrails (CFO)

- **Burn = $0.** No paid APIs for company operation; product fulfillment uses free pipelines
  (Remotion + Edge-TTS + CC media); agent reasoning uses OpenRouter free tier.
- **Floor price.** No plan below $129/mo except the free acquisition tier (1 deliverable).
  Custom builds start at $990.
- **Money-back.** 14-day pilot refund on first month, no questions.
- **Cancel anytime.** No long-term contracts.

---

## 5. Payment

Stripe / Razorpay (INR equivalent on request). Invoices monthly. Enterprise/annual invoicing
on request. _Founder-owned accounts: payment is processed via founder-managed Stripe / Razorpay
accounts. Agents draft invoices and pricing only — no agent collects or holds funds._
